Aspect Model Certificate of Analysis

urn:samm:io.catenax.certificate_of_analysis:1.0.0#CertificateOfAnalysis
The Certificate of Analysis documents the material (e.g. chemical compound) properties describing a material's quality or performance factors specific to manufacturing batch. The exact material properties depend on the material type and the following manufacturing processes. They are defined bilaterally between a supplier (manufacturer) and customer.
