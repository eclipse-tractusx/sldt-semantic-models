Aspect Model Demand and Capacity Notification

urn:samm:io.catenax.demand_and_capacity_notification:1.0.0#DemandAndCapacityNotification
This aspect model defines the standard for demand and capacity notification exchange between business partners. Demand and Capacity Notifications refer to specific events within the supply chain which affect production or demand.
