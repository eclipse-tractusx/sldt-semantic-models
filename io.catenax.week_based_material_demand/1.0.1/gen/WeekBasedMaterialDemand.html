Aspect Model Week-Based Material Demand

urn:samm:io.catenax.week_based_material_demand:1.0.1#WeekBasedMaterialDemand
The requirements of a customer towards a specific supplier for a specific material. Each material demand is unique by its Customer, Supplier and Material Number.
