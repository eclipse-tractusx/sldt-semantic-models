Aspect Model Days of Supply

urn:samm:io.catenax.days_of_supply:2.0.0#DaysOfSupply
Days of Supply refers to the amount of days where item stock is sufficient for the demand. The incoming deliveries are not considered in the main days-of-supply-equation, but they can still be included in the projection of days-of-supply for the future days.

Days Of Supply Allocated to a Partner

This defines the number of days with allocated supply for an item stock in a given location that is available for the use in production or deliveries. The allocated days of supply are not available for other customers.
