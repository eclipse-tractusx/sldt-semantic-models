Aspect Model Manufacturing Capability Aspect Model

urn:samm:io.catenax.manufacturing_capability:3.0.0#ManufacturingCapability
An aspect model representing manufacturing capabilities, based on the concepts for products, processes, resources and capabilities, as well as their relations to each other.
